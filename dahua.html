Dahua NVR Solutions

Dahua Network Video Recorders (NVRs) provide centralized recording and management of video footage. They are compatible with a wide range of cameras, supporting up to 128 channels for large-scale deployments. Key features include:

  • 4K Decoding: Supports real-time 4K video playback and recording.
  • Advanced Compression: Dahua’s H.265+ compression technology reduces storage and bandwidth requirements.
  • Remote Access: Allows users to monitor footage remotely via mobile apps or web browsers.
  • Scalability: Easily expand your system by adding additional cameras and storage as needed.
